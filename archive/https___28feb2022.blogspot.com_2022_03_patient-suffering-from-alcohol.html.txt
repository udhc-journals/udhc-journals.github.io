This is case of a 40 yr old male patient came to casualty with  history of
Involuntary movement of bilateral lower extremity  since 2days

CHIEF COMPLAINT : 

Patient came to casualty on 16/3/22 with involuntary movement of legs since
2days .




DURATIONS : 

GTC type seizure.
Lasting for 3min not a case of uprolling of eyes , tongue bite , involuntary
micturition , 
2 episodes per day .
Was taken to local hospital  and was treated conservatively .




PATIENT HISTORY : 

Patient was asymptomatic 2days back . Then he developed pain in the bilateral
lower extremity .
He fainted when he was in his work and he wasn't conscious until the patient
family brought him to the hospital . He has alcoholic habit from past 20 years
and also has smoking habit  as the same time being.




PRESENT ILLNESS HISTORY :
No similar complaints in the past . No history of head trauma , chestpain ,
palpitations .



HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS : 
No diabetes mellitus and no hyperstension.

Patient is a farmer and does his daily routine activities . He  has stopped his

Later he stopped the habit again until  thn his third son died 6months back .
So he started drinking again. 
Then he was admitted to hospital 2days back.
