giddiness since morning ****
80 years male who is a farmer by occupation came to casuality with chief
complaints of vomiting 2 episodes since morning and giddiness since morning.

HOPI:

atenolol 50 mg since 4 years.

work in field, while working in the field he had sudden onset of giddiness
