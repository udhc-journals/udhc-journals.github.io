This is an online e-log book to discuss our patient's de-identified health data
shared after taking his/her/guardian's informed consent. Here we discuss our
individual patient's problems through series of inputs from available global
online community of experts with an aim to solve those patient's clinical
problems with collective current best evidence based inputs.

CHIEF COMPLAINT:
A 50 yr old female came to OPD with chief complaint of shortness of breath
since 20 days , difficulty in swallowing since 3 days and not able to open
mouth since 1 day

HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS:

Patient was apparently asymptomatic 6 months ago
 2 months back patient had cough and expect oration,pain in the chest region
and went to local hospital and she was diagnosed with pulmonary miliary
tuberculosis 
After 1 month of usage of antitubercular drugs she developed redness and
itching all over the body which is a ATT induced erythroderma.
She stopped ATT for 20 days and then again started using 20 days back.
Patient was apparently alright 20 days back
Patient have Pedal edema, palpitations,chest pain since 3 days
She is having difficulty in Swallowing And since 1 day Patient is not able to
open mouth wide because of pain.
Daily routine:
Before
She used to wake up at 5am and complete her morning household chores till 7am.
She takes a nap in afternoon. Till 9 pm she completes her dinner and sleeps by
10pm. She feel normal tiredness as she's completely engrossed in household
work. 
After
Unfortunately after diagnosis, she's facing difficulty in her daily routine
work. Intially, she can't stand and cook for a longer period of time. Now she's
totally avoiding  work from past 2 months.

HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS:
Known case of rheumatoid arthritis,hypothyroidism, Diabetes and Hypertension
Not a k/c/o Asthama

PERSONAL HISTORY:
Appetite - Normal

Diet- mixed

Bowel movement- regular

Sleep- Adequate

Not alcoholic

GENERAL EXAMINATION:
- patient is Conscious,coherent and cooperative

-pallor is present

-No icterus

-No lymphadenopathy

-No cyanosis

-No clubbing of finger 

VITALS:

Temperature-afebrile
Pulse rate -98bpm
Respiration rate -27bpm
Bp-130/80 mm Hg
Spo -95%

SYSTEMIC EXAMINATION:
◾CVS
- S1 &S2 sounds are heard
- No thrills
- No cardiac murmurs

◾ Respiratory system
Dyspnoea is present

◾CNS 
Sensory and motor system is normal
