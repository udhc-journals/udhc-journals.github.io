I have been given this case to solve in an attempt to understand the topic of
"Patient Clinical Data Analysis" to develop my competency in reading and
comprehending clinical data including history, clinical findings,
investigations and come up with a diagnosis and treatment plan.
Chief complaint:
36 year old male, resident of suryapet came to the OPD with the Chief complaint
 of  increased alcohol consumption since 2 yrs (daily 25units since 2 months)
and increased cigarette smoking since 2 yrs(daily 20 cigarettes)
History of present illness: Patient had habit of consuming alcohol occasionally
2years back ,then he increased the consumption after due to family and
financial stress to 25units
Binge drinking of continuously 15days then stops as he falls He will get
treatment from local RMP nearby, then starts consuming alcohol from the day he
becomes well
Tolerance present
Sleep disturbance,tremors,sweating early in the morning after consuming alcohol
in the night(eye opener present)
Loss of control present
Blood vomitings 3 months back
H/o black colored stools 1 week back
Past history:
N/K/C/O HTN,DM,TB,CAD,ASTHMA
GENERAL EXAMINATION 
Patient is Conscious, coherent,co-operative
No pallor, No icterus, No Cyanosis, No Lymphadenopathy, No Edema

VITALS
Temperature -98F
Pulse rate- 90bpm
Blood pressure-110/70mm hg
Respiratory rate -16cpm

SYSTEMATIC EXAMINATION:
PER ABDOMEN:Soft, non tender
No guarding, No rigidity 
Bowel sounds present 

CARDIOVASCULAR SYSTEM:
S1,S2 heard, NO Murmurs 
RESPIRATORY SYSTEM:
BAE present,NVBS
CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM: NAD

PROVISIONAL DIAGNOSIS-ALCOHOL DEPENDENCE SYNDROME WITH TOBACCO DEPENDENCE
SYNDROME 

INVESTIGATIONS 
LFT:
TB :0.81
DB:0.9
SGOT :29
SGPT:56
ALP: 122
TP:6.6gm/dl
ALBUMIN-3.2gm/dl
A/G-0.96
RBS-76mg/dl
