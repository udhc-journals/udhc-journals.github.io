A 54 years old male came with a chief complaint of renal calculi in right
kidney. 

HISTORY_OF_PRESENT_ILLNESS:-

Patient was apparently asymptomatic 1 year back. Then patient developed pain in
right loin. The he went to the hospital. He was on ayurvedic medicine for few
days. And then he developed pain 1 month back and was admitted in the
hospital. 
He has no history of pyrexia, graveluria, hematuria. 

HISTORY_OF_PAST_ILLNESS:-

Patient has a history of hypertension since 5 years and is in treatment since 4
years. 
Patient has a history of hemorrhoidectomy 4 years back. 
No history of Dm/Tb/epilepsy/asthma.
Patient has a history of right dj stenting under LA 1 month back. 

PERSONAL_HISTORY:-

Diet - mixed
Sleep - normal
Appetite - normal
Addictions - alcohol intake since 10 years
Allergies - no allergies

FAMILY_HISTORY :-

No relevant family history. 

GENERAL_EXAMINATION :-

Patient is examined under well lit room amd adequate ventilation with his
consent taken. 

Patient is conscious, coherent and cooperative, well oriented to time space. He
is moderately built and well nourished. 

No visible pallor, icterus, cyanosis, clubbing, lymphadenopathy, edema.

Vitals :-

Temperature - afebrile
Blood pressure - 130/80 mm Hg
Pulse rate - 83 bpm
Respiration rate - 12 cycles/min

SYSTEMIC_EXAMINATION :-

Cardiovascular_system -
S1 and S2 sounds are heard. 
Respiratory_system -
Central position of trachea. 
No wheeze, no dyspnoea. 
Central_nervous_system -
No neurological defects.
