A 68 year old female came with the chief  complaint of left loin pain since one
week and also with the history of nausea and vomitings 
HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS 
Patient was apparently normal one week back and then later she developed pain
in left loin which was insidious onset and progressive in nature has an history
of nausea and vomitings and also dysuria 
HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS 
Has diabetes and hypertension since one year 
No tb,asthma and epilepsy 
No past surgery 
Hypertension: tab.amiloride 5mg , olmesartan 40mg 
Diabetes mellitus : gemer-1(metformin 500mg ,glimpride1mg)
PHYSICAL EXAMINATION 
TREATMENT HISTORY         
History of diabetes and hypertension 
No known history of asthma,tb, cad,antibiotics hormones,chemo and radiation and
surgeries 
PERSONAL HISTORY 
Married 
Apetite :normal 
Diet:mixed diet 
Bowels:regular 
Micturition:normal 
No known allergies 
No known family history 
VITALS 
Bp:130/70mmhg 
Pulse rate:88bpm
CVS:S1S2
Temperature:98.6F
SYSTEMIC EXAMINATION 
CVS
No thrills , Cardiac murmurs 
Cardiac sounds :S1S2
RESPIRATORY SYSTEM 
No dyspnoea ,wheeze 
Position of trachea: central 
Breathe sounds: vesicular 
ABDOMEN 
Shape of abdomen :scaphoid 
No tenderness, palpable mass, free fluid , bruits 
Hernial orifice : normal 
Liver,spleen not palpable 
CNS
Conscious,normal,no neck stiffness ,no kernings sign

Left proximal upper uretric calculi
