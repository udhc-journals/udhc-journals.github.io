DATE OF ADMISSION:24/02/2022

Chief complaint 

55year old female patient presented to OPD with the chief complaints of blurred
vision with itching of left eye, from past 4 days

HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS:

The patient was apparently asymptomatic 1 years back then she developed blurred
vision .

Initially it was normal then she developed dov in the left eye 
It was small at first but increased later


PAST HISTORY:The patient had undergone cataract to the right eye 2yrs back

She is also a know case of diabetes since 5 years and is on medication. 

She also has bronchial asthama since 20 years and is on medication

No history of hypertension,tuberculosis 

No history of epilepsy

No history of CAD


 

PERSONAL HISTORY:

appetite : normal.

Diet : mixed.

Bowels : irregular

Micturition : normal

FAMILY HISTORY:

no relevant family history is seen.

TREATMENT HISTORY:
She is on medication for diabetes for 5 years
And on medication for asthama for 20 years

GENERAL EXAMINATION:

The patient was coherent, conscious, cooperative, well orientation to time ,
place and person.

No icterus

No pallor

No cyanosis

No lymphadenopathy

No clubbing

No edema .

VITALS:

Temperature : afebrile

BP : 170/120mmhg

Pulse: 90bpm

Respiration :18cpm

SPO2 at room 98%
