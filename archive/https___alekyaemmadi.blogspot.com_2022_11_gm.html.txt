 I have been given this case to solve in an attempt to understand the topic of
patient clinical data analysis to develop my competency in reading and
comprehending clinical data including, history, clinical
findings,investigations,and come up with a diagnosis and treatment plan.

1week

Vomitings since 10 days

Vomitings:- 4-5 episodes, non projectile, non bilious,non foul smelling
contents include food particles,Which subsided on taking medication.
Fever was incidious in onset, associated with chills and rigor,which subsided
on taking medication.

10years back,patient's mother observed swelling over the neck and was diagnosed
with HYPOTHYROIDISM and was on TAB.THYROXINE SODIUM 25mcg

taking medication.

dysmenorrhea,with clots.
