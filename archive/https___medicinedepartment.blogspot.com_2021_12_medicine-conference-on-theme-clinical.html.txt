**** Medicine conference on the theme, "Clinical problem solving" 2022 ****
Medicine Conference with the theme "Clinical problem solving 2022" focuses on
the idea of ‘patient centered medical cognition’ and features fascinating and
inspiring content from speakers supported by global experts, covering key areas
in medicine. 

The conference is aimed to  serve as a platform for the medicine community to
share best practices, acquire knowledge, and debate innovative approaches for
"clinical problem solving" together.
