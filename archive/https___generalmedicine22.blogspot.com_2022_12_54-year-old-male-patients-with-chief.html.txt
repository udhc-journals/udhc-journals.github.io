CHIEF COMPLAINTS: C/o of hiccups and generalized weakness since 1 week

HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS: 
Patient was apparently asymptomatic and developed hiccups 7 days backs
Similar complaints 8 months and 4 months backs, 
and 1 month back H/o trauma to head

HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS:
Patient was apparently asymptomatic 7 years back and then had a h/o trauma to
foot which was not healing then went to hospital then diagnosed as diabetes
mellitus since 7 years
Pt is a k/c/o HTN Since 1 month
Pt is not a k/c/o tuberculosis, asthma, CAD, CVA

PERSONAL HISTORY:
married
Diet mixed, appetite lost
Sleep disturbed
Bowel and bladder movements-regular
No other habits

GENERAL EXAMINATION
No signs of pallor, icterus, cyanosis, clubbing and pedal edema

VITALS

Pulse rate-80bpm
RR-18cpm
BP-130/90
GRBS-88mg%

SYSTEMIC EXAMINATION
RS- bae+ ,normal vesicular breath sounds 
Cvs-S1 S2 +, no murmurs heard
Abdomen-scaphoid

PROVISIONAL DIAGNOSIS
Dyselectroltemia

TREATMENT
*IV fluids NS@UO+30ml/hr
*Inj pan 40mg IV OD
*Inj optineuron 1 amp in 100 ml NS IV/OD
*Syp potchlor 15ml in 1 glass of water PO/TID
*Inj.kcl 3 amp (60 mea)+1 amp MGSO4 (2gm) in 500 ml NS @ 100 ml/hr

*Tab Cinod 10 mg PO/OD 8) Strict diabetic diet
