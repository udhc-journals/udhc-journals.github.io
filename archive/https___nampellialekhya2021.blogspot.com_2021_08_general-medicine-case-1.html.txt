A 53yr old female resident of basireddypally,daily wage worker by occupation
came to our hospital with complaints of shortness of breath,decreased urine
output  and weakness since 6months 
HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS 
Patient was apparently asymptomatic 10years back.
As per the patient she suffered from severe backpain,breathlessness and
weakness.Then she consulted a doctor in Hyderabad and he diagnosed her as
chronic kidney disease 
She was on medication from past 10years which were prescribed by the doctor 
6months back she noticed decreased urine output and shortness of breath.Then
she visited our hospital and was admitted into dialysis ward.She was on
dialysis since 6months once in 3days.After dialysis patient complaints of fever
with chills.
PAST HISTORY 
She is a known case of hypertension since 10years.she is on medication since
10years.she has 4 vaginal deliveries and tubectomised 25years back

She is a vegetarian from 10years

Inadequate sleep
