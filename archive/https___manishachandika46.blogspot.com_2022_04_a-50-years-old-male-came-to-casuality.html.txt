A 50 years old male who is a farmer came to casuality with knee pain since 1
year. The pain was insidious in onset and is gradually progressive. 

HISTORY_OF_PRESENT_ILLNESS :-

Patient was apparently asymptomatic 4 years back. Then he developed knee pain.
The pain was insidious in onset and is gradually progressive. He has severe
knee pain and pedal edema since 6 days and was admitted in the hospital. He was
diagnosed with hyperuricemia and anemia under evaluation. 

He has no history of diarhoea and vomitings. 
He has no history shortness of breath. 

HISTORY_OF_PAST_ILLNESS :-

No history of alcohol and smoking. 
No history of hypertension and diabetes mellitus. 

PERSONAL_HISTORY:-

Diet - mixed
Sleep - normal
Appetite - decreased
Addictions - no history of alcohol
Allergies - no allergies

FAMILY_HISTORY :-
 
No relevant family history. 

GENERAL_EXAMINATION :-

Patient is examined under well lit room amd adequate ventilation with his
consent taken. 

Patient is conscious, coherent and cooperative, well oriented to time space. He
is moderately built and well nourished. 

No visible pallor, icterus, cyanosis, clubbing, lymphadenopathy. 
History of pedal edema since 6 days.
