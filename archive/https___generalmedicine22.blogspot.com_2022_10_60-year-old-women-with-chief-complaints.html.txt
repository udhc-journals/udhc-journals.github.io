Loss of appetite since 1 month

HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS:

Patient is not a k/c/o dm, epilepsy, asthma, cad
H/O 2 PRBC transfusion 10 to 15 days back

PERSONAL HISTORY :
Diet- mixed
Appetite-lost
Sleep-disturbed
Bowel and bladder movements- regular
Addiction : No addictions, 
Consumes palm wine occasionally

FAMILY HISTORY: 
Not significant

GENERAL EXAMINATION:
No Pallor , icterus, cyanosis, clubbing,lymphadenopathy, edema

SYSTEMIC EXAMINATION:
CVS: S1,S2 heard, no murmurs heard 
P/A: Soft, Non-tender
CNS: No abnormalities seen.

PROVISIONAL DIAGNOSIS:
Chronic renal failure
