May 17, 2020

Hello. This is Raveena Reddy. I'm a medicine intern and I'm here to share my
daily experiences here as a part of my internship completion.

Today's case : 


A 45 year old male came to opd with chief complaints of decreased appetite
since 1 month and loose stools since 20 days


HOPI 

Patient was apparently asymptomatic 1 month back then developed decreased
appetite , Increased chest discomfort immediately on taking food , gradually
progressed

Loose stools since 20 days watery consistency, normal colour  4 episodes per
day not blood stained patient. but, now complains of blood stained stools 1
episode per day normal consistency since 2 days after using medication 

Patient complains of generalized weakness and not able to do daily activities 

Belching present 

No h/o fever, vomitings, pain abdomen,chestpain,cough ,sob.


Past history: 

K/c/o HIV since 9 years

On regular medication TLE regimen

CD4+ count= 500cells/cu.mm on 4-07-2019
