D/O/admission:22-4-2022

CHIEF COMPLAINT:
A 14 year old female came to OPD with a complaint of Fever and vomitings since
2 days

HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS:
Patient was apparently asymptomatic 2 days back then she developed fever Which
was high grade, intermittent associated with 3 episodes of Vomitings
(immediately after taking food)Which are non-bilious,non-Projectile.
She was taken to a local RMP doctor 2 days back When she had high grade fever
for which she was given paracetomol.
The next day She was taken to Local hospital since the fever hadn't subsided
and was given Inj•Arteether,Tab cefopodoxime and Tab•Dolo which fever had
Touched the baseline
No H/O Rash,Malena,Bleeding manifestations

HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS:
Not a K/C/O HTN,DM,TB,COPD

PERSONAL HISTORY:
Appetite: Lost
Diet : mixed
Bowel and bladder moments : regular
No allergies 
No addictions

GENERAL EXAMINATION:
Patient is conscious, coherent and cooperative

-No clubbing
-No oedema

Spo -98%
