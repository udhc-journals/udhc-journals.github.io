Here is the case I have seen-

of cracked nipples since 7 days. breathlessness and profuse sweating since 3
days, vomitings since morning.

profuse sweating and breathlessness since 3 days,sob is sudden in onset and
progressed from grade 1-4. Sweating n sob increases after breast feeding her
baby.H/O 5 episodes of loose stools 4 days back.she underwent LSCS 45 days back
and c/o bleeding PV since 45 days.
No past h/o of HTN, DM, COPD,TB, epilepsy, jaundice ,CHD, CKD
