Since 1day patient had facial puffiness and breathless for which he went to a
hospital.
HISTORY OF PAST ILLNESS:
Patient had knee joint pain 4years and low back pain since 3years for which she
had taken nsaids and ointments, pateint also had pedal edema upto knee.
